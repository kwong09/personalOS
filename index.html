kayla's boba log

explore kayla's boba log to see the various boba shops she's visited and how she rates them

gong cha

rating: 10000/10
order: pearl milk tea, 100% sweet, less ice

why do i love this so much? basically, the boba is the perfect density and not too hard and not too chewy. also, the price is not too expensive because my almost-empty wallet can afford this boba. lastly, the sweetness is absolutely perfect and the milk tea is not too strong and it is not too fragrant (unlike jasmine and oolong)

7 leaves

rating: 4/5
order: oolong milk tea with boba

in my opinion, this milk tea was kind of stronger, like less fragrant than jasmine but like 'coffee' kind of strong. not my favorite, but it wasn't too bad. the boba was good!

vending machine (at school)

rating: 3/5
order: jasmine milk tea with some weirdjelly

i don't really like jasmine milk tea and the 'boba' in it was not... chewy. it was super tough to chew. so, yeah. but the milk tea tasted better as i got used to it. wouldn't recommend to anyone though, lol

basketball

introduction

when kayla was in 2nd grade she decided to play basketball. why? i'm not sure. maybe because her friends were playing or maybe because her parents encouraged her to. either way, this was the origin story of kayla's basketball journey and the hardships that came along with it. sroll more to learn more about kayla and basketball :)

high school

fortunately, basketball is not taken too seriously at my school. i have much more fun because it feels like i'm just playing a sport with my friends. it's much less stressful and more enjoyable for me!!

am i good enough?

"am i good enough?" this question haunts many people, causing people to doubt themselves and lose confidence. to this day, it is something that i still struggle with. when i played basketball i would constantly compare myself to other people around me. my friends played for national teams and i live in an area where basketball is taken very seriously. to this day, i always ask myself this question. it has made me question the real purpose of why i am playing basketball. am i doing it for the fun of it? are others forcing me to play? am i doing it so when my friends ask me to play i won't look like a dumb dumb? i'm still not sure.
